Basketball is a low contact sport played on a rectangular court. While most professional basketball games connist of five players on each team, three-on-three, two-on-two, and one-on-one competitions are also popular. The objective is to shoot a basketball through a hoop. A hoop is mounted to a backboard at each end of the court. The game was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith.
