The Court

The professional NBA courts are 94 feet long, 50 feet wide and the hoop is elevated 10 above the ground.In the NBA if you score inside the three point arc it counts as two points but if you score outside the three point arc it counts as three points.If you travel outside of the court while you have the ball it is out of bounds and it will be the other teams ball.

Just The Basic Rules

1.While you have the ball you can not take more than two steps without dribbling or else the ref will call a travel

2.When you dribble the ball you can not hold the ball from under it. When you dribble you hand must be on top of the ball or else the ref will call carry

3.You can not double dribble. Double dribble is when you dribble the ball wit two hands

4.You can not jump and land while the ball is in your hand or else that is an up and down
